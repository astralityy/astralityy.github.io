The Astral Crusaders





About

The Astral Crusaders were founded on June 19th, 1987 by Theodore Daridian. The organization was passed down to his son, Jonnathan "Jimmy" Daridian.

The organization's main goal is to maintain the pursuit of knowledge and happiness within society amongst distractions such as social media and television.

Principals

The five principals of the Astral Crusaders as as follows:

1. Knowledge is wealth

2. Happiness is eternal

3. Exploration is never complete

4. Believe in the soul

5. Do not fear death
